    a blessing in disguise /ˈbles.ɪŋ/
    /dɪsˈɡaɪz/    		 trong cái rủi có cái may, điều mà thoạt tiên mình không nhận ra, cứ tưởng đó là điều không tốt, nhưng cuối cùng hóa ra đó lại là một điều tốt
    - something that at first seems bad, but later turns out to be beneficial
    - an unfortunate event or situation that results in an unforeseen positive outcome
  • Missing the train was a blessing in disguise, for if I hadn't, I wouldn't have met my future wife.
  • Being laid off was a blessing in disguise – within a month I got a much better job.
