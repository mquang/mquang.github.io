petty /ˈpet̬.i/ (adj)
1. nhỏ nhặt, không quan trọng, không nghiêm trọng
  • small or of little importance and not worth giving attention to
  • (of a crime or criminal) not very serious


    • 2. nhỏ mọn; luôn để ý đến những điều nhỏ nhặt, không đáng kể; hay chấp nhặt (selfish and mean, esp. because of having too much interest in small and unimportant matters; complaining too much about things that are not important)
    		 1.
    (syn) trivial
  • I don’t have time for petty matters like that.
  • The officer did not arrest the teen for the petty crime.

    • 2.
  • We started having arguments over petty little things.
  • The sudden rivalries and petty jealousies.
  • How could you be so petty?
