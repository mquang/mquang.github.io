        One year ago, I moved to Boston with my boyfriend Oliver and our dog Sammy. I was excited to start my new life with my man in the big city. We hired a charming realtor — named Jessica, and she took us to hunt for a dream apartment. The first four options were horrible: small, dirty, dark, and noisy. But when I saw the fifth apartment, I fell in love! It was affordable and had a lovely spacious closet. We signed the contract that same day and moved in.

        While Oliver moved all of our stuff with the movers, I had a little chat with our neighbor, Mia. She was a 27-year-old freelance writer who lived alone with lots of plants. When we unpacked our bags and boxes, Mia knocked on the door and brought a flower in a pot as a welcome gift. We invited her in for coffee and she gave us a lot of useful tips about the laundry room in the basement, the Wi-Fi, the garbage, and other things like that… Mia also said that I had great taste and suggested we go shopping together some day.

        The next month was like heaven. Oliver and I were getting used to living together in the big city. We found furniture and decor items at flea markets and decorated our little nest. Then one day I took Mia shopping and she was acting very weird… She complimented me the whole time, which was annoying. When she suggested we buy the same clothes to look like twins, I thought it was a joke… But it wasn’t! The next morning I went for a run as usual, and Mia joined me, even though she wasn't invited. I wanted to tell her that I liked to run alone, but I hesitated… And she began to join me every morning! On top of that, every time we went for a walk with Sammy, she would be waiting for us outside accidentally.

In the middle of the party, she texted me: “Hey, whatcha doin’?” I didn't wanna lie, so I just ignored the message. After 30 minutes, everyone smelled burning and saw smoke coming from the corridor and Mia’s apartment. I was terrified! Thank God we had smoke alarms in the building! Oliver knocked on Mia’s door… She opened and apologized. When we returned and I went to Mia's to pick the plants up. When she opened the door, I saw that she had cut and dyed her hair to look exactly like me… Her clothes looked exactly like my clothes. Even the jewelry was similar to my style. The interior of her apartment also changed, she painted her walls purple and decorated them with Moroccan rugs, just like I did. I took the plants and told her that I had to run… At the moment I realized that something was wrong with this girl…

And I yelled at her, saying that she was acting like a crazy person by trying to copy my style. After that short fight I blocked her number and began to ignore her when she knocked on our door.

Unfortunately Mia owned her apartment and he could not force her to move out. Then I told Oliver that we should move out, but we had signed a lease agreement for one year and we didn't have money for relocation…
