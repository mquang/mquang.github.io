in vain /veɪn/ vô ích, không có kết quả
  • without success in spite of your efforts
  • without producing a good or desired result

    		•
  • All the police's efforts to find him were in vain.
  • Martha looked bewildered as she tried in vain to quiet Claire.
  • Altman swore that his son’s death would not be in vain.
  • Her suffering will not be in vain.
