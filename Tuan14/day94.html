redundant /rɪˈdʌn.dənt/ (adj)
1. thừa
(especially of a word, phrase, etc.) unnecessary because it is more than is needed

2. mất việc		 1.
  • In the sentence "She is a single unmarried woman", the word "unmarried" is redundant.
  • I think this phrase is a little redundant.
  • redundant code
    • (phrase) made redundant: if you are made redundant, you lose your job because your employer no longer you
  • To keep the company alive, half the workforce is being made redundant.
  • He was worried about being made redundant at work.
  • More than 200 of the company's employees have already been made redundant.
    • Phân biệt being fired (bị sa thải, bị đuổi) với being made redundant (bị cho nghỉ vì công việc bạn đang làm không còn cần thiết cho lợi ích của công ty)
