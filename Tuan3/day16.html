  • Chris deserves our special thanks for all his efforts.
  • They deserve praise for all their hard work.
  • Nobody deserves this more than you.
  • You don't deserve that.
  • I hope they get the punishment they deserve.

    severe /səˈvɪr/ (adj) (VERY SERIOUS)
  • khắt khe, nghiêm trọng, gắt (thái độ)
  • gay go, dữ dội, khốc liệt
  • khắc nghiệt (thời tiết)
    		•
  • severe weather conditions
  • His injury severe.
  • The weather became very severe.
  • My uncle became severe when I was late.
  • She was a severe woman who seldom smiled.
  • (n) severity: mức độ nghiêm trọng
  • The chances of a full recovery will depend on the severity of her injuries.
  • Even the doctors were shocked by the severity of his injuries.
  • I don't think you quite understand the severity of our financial problems.

    serene /səˈrin/ (adj) thanh thản, thanh bình (calm, peaceful, tranquil, relaxed)
  • During the test, I tried to remain serene so I would not get nervous and make careless mistakes.
  • The mother played serene music as her baby slept.
  • Taking yoga classes helps me remain serene.
  • Standing in front of the waterfall, the bride and groom made a serene picture.
  • Although the children are gone for two days, my husband and I cannot enjoy a serene weekend because of the neighbor’s noisy dog.
  • (n) serenity: sự thanh thản, sự thanh bình (sóng yên biển lặng)
  • He wrote of the beauty and serenity of the great river.
