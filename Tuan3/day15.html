sequence /ˈsi.kwəns/ (n)
1. sự nối tiếp (theo thứ tự)

2. chuỗi sự kiện/hành động (xảy ra theo trình tự, thứ tự)


3. dãy (số, kí tự)
  • Number the pages in sequence.
  • We go to the classroom in sequence.
  • the sequence of events leading up to the war
  • My daughter has been rehearsing a dance sequence for her school play for the last three weeks.
  • This is the standard ASCII sequence.
  • The computer generates a random sequence of numbers.
  • (adj) sequential: tuần tự
  • sequential data processing

    consequence /ˈkɑn.sə.kwəns/ (n)
    1. hậu quả, kết quả


    2. quan trọng, nghiêm trọng    		 1.
  • Your lies have resulted in consequences.
  • His decisions serious consequences.
  • They are facing serious consequences.

    • 2.
  • The money was of little consequence to Tony.
  • Don't worry. It's of no consequence.
  • Last summer there were 15 hurricanes, but only one was of any consequence.
  • (idiom) in consequence (of something): do kết quả của, do hậu quả của
  • In consequence of the storm, there was no electricity.
  • The child was born deformed in consequence of an injury to its mother.
  • She lost a lot of money in consequence of some risky investments.
  • (adv) consequently: do đó (THEREFORE)
  • All the shops were closed, and consequently we couldn't buy any food.
  • I spent most of my money in the first week and consequently had very little to eat by the end of the holiday.
  • I was very worried, and consequently I couldn’t concentrate.

    regulation /ˌrɛ.ɡyəˈleɪ.ʃn/ (n) quy định, quy tắc, điều lệ
    (adj) theo quy tắc, theo quy định
  • regulations
  • New safety regulations have been brought in.
  • It's regulation to wear suits at the office.
  • She was wearing the regulation school uniform.
  • (v) regulate: điều chỉnh, chỉnh đốn, sửa lại cho đúng, quy định
  • Their parents regulate how much TV the children can watch.
  • This system can regulate the temperature of the room.
  • The drug helps to regulate Ryan's heartbeat.
  • (v) regularize: làm theo đúng quy tắc, hợp thức hóa, chính quy hóa
  • Some people want to regularize the English spelling system (= change it so that all words follow the same rules for spelling).
  • Under the program, illegal immigrants would be able to apply to regularize their status.
