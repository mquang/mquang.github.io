real estate agent /ˈriː.əl ɪ.steɪt ˌeɪ.dʒənt/ (n, US) người môi giới bất động sản, người đại lý bất động sản (a person whose job is to sell houses and land for people)
synonym: (UK) estate agent
  • She's a real estate agent.
  • The real estate agent makes money by commission.
  • We found our house through a real estate agent.
  • (n) estate: tài sản (địa ốc, nhà đất)
  • Mrs. Graham left her entire estate to her three children.
  • (n) real estate: bất động sản
  • (n) agent:
    1. người đại diện, người đại lý     (REPRESENTATIVE)

    2. đặc vụ
    • 1.
  • Please contact our agent in Spain for further information.
  • Our agent in New York deals with all US sales.
  • I made the booking through a travel agent.

    • 2.
  • an agent
  • (n) agency: đại lý, cơ quan, văn phòng (a business that provides a particular service for people or organizations)
  • Ms.Ife Bankole was the manager of an employment agency in Lagos for five years.
  • a car rental agency
  • an advertising agency
  • a travel agency

  • abbreviation (HR)

    social /ˈsoʊ.ʃl/ (adj) có tính xã hội, mang tính xã hội (connected with society)
  • social
  • It took a while to get used to the social nuances of the office.
  • (adj) sociable: dễ gần, thích giao du, thích kết bạn (Sociable people like to meet and spend time with other people)
    => (n) sociability
    => (adj) unsociable
  • Rob's very sociable - he likes parties.
  • I don't mean to be unsociable, but I'm tired.
  • Do you think Tom is unsociable?
  • (n) society: xã hội
  • What is the role of the University in the modern society?
  • (v) socialize: giao lưu kết hợp (to meet and spend time with people in a friendly way, in order to enjoy yourself)
  • Maybe you should socialize more.
  • They don't socialize with their neighbors much.
  • I enjoy socializing with the other students.

    2. riêng, chỉ có ở (existing only in one place or limited to one thing)    		 1.

  • She didn't give specific .
  • The money was collected for a specific purpose.

    • 2.
