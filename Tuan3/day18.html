Từ / Cụm từ Phiên âm Nghĩa Ví dụ
special /ˈspɛ.ʃl/ (adj) đặc biệt
  • Our special guest on next week's show will be…
  • Is there anything special that you'd like to do today?
  • I'm cooking something special for her birthday.
  • I have a suit for special occasions.
  • Her voice is OK, but it's nothing special.
  • (n) specialist: chuyên gia, chuyên môn, chuyên khoa
  • a specialist
  • Dr. Washington is a specialist in genetics.
  • My doctor referred me to a hospital specialist.



  • (v) specialize: chuyên sâu, chuyên về (mảng nào đó)
    concentrate on and become expert in a particular subject or skill
  • I enjoy working in general medicine, but I hope to be able to specialize in the future.
  • The company specializes in customized computer systems.
  • a restaurant that specializes in seafood
  • Many students prefer not to specialize too soon.
  • He specialized in criminal law.
  • (adv) specially: đặc biệt vì 1 mục đích cụ thể, cho một người cụ thể (for a particular purpose, person, etc.)
  • The ring was specially made for her.
  • We came specially to see you.
  • We bought the chair specially for Grandma when she visits.
  • The uniform was specially designed for women.
  • The suit was specially made for me.
    		•
    especially /ɪˈspɛ.ʃli/ (adv)
    1. đặc biệt là, nhất là (dùng để chỉ một điều gì đó mà bạn nói đến có sự đặc biệt nhiều hơn những thứ khác)

    2. rất, cực kỳ (very much)
    3. đặc biệt cho một mục đích cụ thể nào đó
  • I love Rome, especially in the spring.
  • I love Australian wines, especially the white wines.
    • YouTube: Mat Franco
  • My mom has been an especially important person in my life.
  • He especially appreciated her help.
  • The mountain climb is especially dangerous.
  • I made it especially for you.
  • I chose this especially for your new house.
  • The uniform was especially designed for women.
    • Phân biệt especially và specially:
    Especially và specially đều là trạng từ.
    Especially mang nghĩa đặc biệt là, nhất là (dùng để chỉ một điều gì đó mà bạn nói đến có sự đặc biệt nhiều hơn những thứ khác)
    Specially nói đến đặc biệt cho một mục đích cụ thể
    Especially cũng có thể mang nghĩa đặc biệt cho một mục đích cụ thể
    particular /pərˈtɪ.kyə.lər/ (adj)
    1. cụ thể
    2. kỹ lưỡng, tường tận, cặn kẽ, câu nệ đến từng chi tiết, kén (ăn, mặc)
    Từ này mình thấy được dùng để giải nghĩa cho các từ khác trên từ điển Oxford với Cambridge nhiều lắm, bạn chú ý nhé
  • I need a particular example of what you are talking about.
  • She's very particular about .
  • Don't worry, Mom. He isn't particular about food. He eats anything.
  • He's very particular about the kitchen - everything has to be perfectly clean and in its place.
  • (idiom) in particular:
    1. đặc biệt là, nhất là (especially)
    2. đặc biệt (special)

    • 1.
  • I liked the last candidate in particular.
  • He loves science fiction in particular.
  • I am interested in playing outdoor sports. Cricket, in particular.

    • 2.
  • Is there anything in particular you'd like for dinner?
  • Are you going anywhere in particular?
  • What are you doing tonight?’ - ‘Nothing in particular.’
    		•
    peculiar /pɪˈkyu.lyər/ (adj)
    1. dị, lạ (thường là mang lại cảm giác khó chịu, đáng lo ngại) (unusual and strange, especially in a way that is unpleasant or worrying)

    2. riêng, đặc thù (thuộc về hay liên quan đến một vùng cụ thể nào đó)
    (belonging or relating to one particular place, situation, person, etc., and not to others)    		 1
  • a peculiar smell/taste
  • What a peculiar smell!
  • This sauce smells really peculiar.
  • There was a peculiar smell in the kitchen.
  • For some peculiar reason, she refused to come inside.
  • He looked at me with a very peculiar expression.
  • Seeing blood makes me feel a bit peculiar.

    • 2.
  • This type of building is peculiar to the south of the country.
  • This style of cooking is peculiar to the South-Eastern provinces.
  • The problem of racism is not peculiar to this country.
