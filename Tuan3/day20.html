Từ / Cụm từ Phiên âm Nghĩa Ví dụ
occur /əˈkər/ (v) xảy ra, xuất hiện (đặc biệt là tai nạn hoặc sự kiện không mong muốn...)
  • If any of these symptoms occur while you are taking the medication, consult your doctor immediately.
  • An explosion occurred there.
  • The earthquake occurred in the morning.
  • Many suicides occur in prisons.
  • (n) occurrence:
    1. sự việc/sự cố/chuyện xảy ra

    2. sự xuất hiện
  • Domestic violence is a regular occurrence in some families.
  • The program counts the number of occurrences of any word within the text.
  • The occurrence of storms delayed our trip.
    		•
    produce (v): /prəˈdus/
    (n): /ˈprɑː.duːs/    		 (v) sản xuất (to make things to be sold, especially in large quantities)
    (n) sản vật, sản phẩm (food or any other substance or material that is grown or obtained through farming)
    		 - agricultural produce: nông sản
    - dairy produce: sản phẩm từ sữa
  • How many cars do they produce annually?
  • The show is produced by his company.
    • YouTube: Learn English with Bob the Canadian
  • (n) producer:
    + người sản xuất, nhà sản xuất (phim, âm nhạc...)
    + doanh nghiệp/nước sản xuất     (a company, country, or person that provides goods, especially those that are produced by an industrial process or grown or obtained through farming, usually in large amounts)
  • They have a talented producer.
  • Australia is one of the world's main producers of wool.
  • China is the world's leading producer of rice.
  • São Paulo is the biggest producer of eggs in the country.
  • (v) reproduce: tái hiện lại (việc tạo ra/tìm ra)
  • You should only report a bug when you can reproduce it.
  • You should report a bug even if it is not reproducible.
  • We were unable to reproduce the results of the original experiment.
    		•
    product /ˈprɑ.dʌkt/ (n)
    1. sản phẩm (something that is made to be sold, usually something that is produced by an industrial process or, less commonly, something that is grown or obtained through farming)


    2. tích số (the result you get when two or more numbers are multiplied together)
    		 1.
  • I bought some skincare products for her.
  • My company online products.
  • How can we our products?
  • The company is concentrating on developing new products.

    • 2.
  • The product of 6 and 3 is 18.
  • (n) production:
    1. sản phẩm, tác phẩm

    2. quy trình sản xuất     (the process of making or growing goods to be sold)

    3. sản lượng (the quantity of goods that is produced)
  • Disney's latest production (= film) looks likely to be its most successful ever.
  • Steel production is estimated to have reached 100 million tons last year.
  • Coke is used in the production of steel.
  • The car went out of production in 2007.



  • a decline/an increase in production
  • UK wheat production is forecast to be 700,000 tonnes higher than last year.
    		•
    productivity /ˌprɒd.ʌkˈtɪv.ə.t̬i/ (n) năng suất làm việc (the rate at which a worker, a company or a country produces goods, and the amount produced, compared with how much time, work and money is needed to produce them)
  • Studies show that if a working environment is pleasant, productivity increases.
  • Coffee always seems to increase my productivity and helps me make it through the day.
  • (adj) productive: năng suất (producing or achieving a lot)
  • We had a very productive meeting – a lot of problems were solved.
  • I had a very productive day.
    		•
    provide /prəˈvaɪd/ (v) cung cấp (to give someone something that they need)
  • She provided the for camping.
  • Please answer questions in the space provided.
  • Sheep provide us with wool.
  • Cows provide us with milk.
  • I will provide you all the necessary information.
  • (phrasal verb) provide for:
    1. chu cấp, lo liệu cho cái ăn, cái mặc     (to look after someone by making money in order to buy the things that they need)

    2. chuẩn bị, đề phòng, dự phòng những tình huống/sự kiện có thể xảy ra trong tương lai (to make plans/preparations in order to deal with a possible event in the future)
    • 1.
  • He worked hard to provide for his family.
  • He has to provide for.

    • 2.
  • She provided for her old age.
  • We must provide for depreciation when calculating the costs.
  • Commanders failed to provide for an attack by sea.
    • provided that: miễn là (ngày 4)
  • (n) provision:
    1. sự cung cấp

    2. sự chuẩn bị, dự phòng
    • 1.
  • Very few companies make childcare provision for working mothers.

    • 2.
  • When designing buildings in this area, you have to make provision against earthquakes.
  • You need to make adequate provision for your retirement.
    		•
    supply /səˈplaɪ/ (n) sự cung cấp, sự tiếp tế; đồ dự trữ, hàng hóa dự trữ (an amount of something that is provided or available to be used)
    (v) cung cấp (to provide something that is wanted or needed, often in large quantities and over a long period of time)
  • The water supply is unsafe.
  • Have you checked our supplies recently?
  • Why don't you go and buy some supplies?
  • Electrical power is supplied by underground cables.
  • We supply systems for clients on the Pacific rim.
  • The school refused to supply more materials for the classroom.
  • Cows supply milk.
  • Cows supply us with milk.
  • The Red Cross supplied the hospital with blood.
    • supply and demand: cung và cầu
