rebellious /rɪˈbeli.əs/ (adj) chống đối, nổi loạn, nghịch ngợm
  • rebellious teenagers
  • (n, v) rebel:
    (n1) (đứa trẻ) chống đối, nghịch ngợm     (a person who does not like rules or authority, and shows this by behaving differently from most people in society)

    (n2) kẻ chống đối (lại chính quyền, chính sách, luật pháp...), quân nổi loạn, quân phiến loạn (a person who fights against the government of their country)
    (v) chống đối
    • (n1)
  • I've always been the rebel of the family.
  • He was a rebel when he was a teenager and dyed his hair pink.

    • (n2)
  • rebel forces
  • The army recaptured the town from the rebels.

    • (v)
  • If you are too strict with teenagers, they often rebel.

    slap /slæp/ (n) cái tát, cú vả
    (v) tát, vả, đập (tay), vỗ vào lưng (to hit someone or something with the flat part of the hand or other flat object)
    		 (n)

    • (v)
  • ‘Congratulations!’ he said, slapping me on the back.
  • He slapped the newspaper down on the desk.
  • When her ideas were rejected, she slapped her report (down) on the table and stormed out of the meeting.

    freak /friːk/ (n) người dị hợm, lập dị (someone who is considered to be very strange because of the way they look, behave, or think)
    (adj)
    (of an event or the weather) very unusual and unexpected    		 (n)
    (syn) weirdo
  • I was born with black hair all over my back, like some sort of freak.
  • At my school you were considered to be a freak if you weren't interested in sport.
  • These glasses make me look like a freak.

    • (adj)
  • He was crushed to death in a freak accident.
  • The men drowned when a freak wave sank their boat.

    piercing /ˈpɪə.sɪŋ/ (n) lỗ khuyên (the hole that is made in your ear, nose or some other part of your body so that you can wear jewellery there)
  • He has several tattoos and multiple piercings.
  • She has a tongue piercing.
  • (v) pierce: xỏ khuyên, đục lỗ, đâm thủng, xuyên qua (to make a small hole in something, or to go through something, with a sharp object)
  • I couldn't wear these earrings because my ears aren't pierced.
  • The arrow pierced his shoulder.

    hysterical /hɪˈsterɪ.kəl/ (adj)
    1. trạng thái hưng phấn tâm lý quá mức, không điều khiển được cảm xúc (unable to control your behaviour or emotions because you are very upset, afraid, excited etc)

    2. hài hước (extremely funny)    		 1.

  • hysterical screams
  • Tom laughed hysterically for no reason.
