Từ / Cụm từ Phiên âm Nghĩa Ví dụ
According to /əˈkɔːrdɪŋ tə/
/əˈkɔːrdɪŋ tu/		 (preposition) theo như, theo
Từ này hay xuất hiện trong Part 3, Part 4, Part 7
  • According to the letter, what will most likely happen in April?
  • According to the speaker, what information is avaiable on a Web site?
  • According to Jeff, the film starts at 7.30.
    • According to thường đứng đầu câu, theo sau bởi 1 cụm danh từ hoặc 1 mệnh đề
    in order to (liên từ phụ thuộc - subordinating conjunction) để, để mà
  • in order to do something hay viết khác là in order to + V

  • He came home early in order to see the children before they went to bed.
  • I am planning to move house in order to be closer to my place of work.
  • I'm going to Ireland in order to visit my family.
  • What do I have to do in order to convince them?
    		•
    in place of (phrase) thay cho, thay vì (instead of)
  • I changed my mind. I want a red one in place of the blue one.
  • You can use milk in place of cream in this recipe.
    		•
    by means of (phrase)
    1. do, bởi, nhờ (Due to)
    2. bằng ... (using something; with the use of something)    		 1.
  • I only wake up in time for work by means of an alarm clock.
  • I was able to get an A in my math class this semester by means of hard work.
  • They succeeded by means of patience and sacrifice.
  • By means of you I understand the meaning of love.

    • 2.
  • I opened the bottle by means of a bottle opener.
  • The trailer was connected to the car by means of a complicated system of hoses, pipes and rods.
    		•
    In accordance with /ɪn əˈkɔːdns wɪθ/ (idiom) đúng với, theo như (in a way that agrees with or follows (something, such as a rule or request)
    in accordance with a rule, law, wish, etc.
  • In accordance with her wishes, she was buried in France.
  • I did this in accordance with your request.
  • In accordance with your request, I am sending a copy of my book.
  • His funeral will be private, in accordance with his wishes.
