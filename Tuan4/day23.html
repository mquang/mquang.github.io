consult /kənˈsʌlt/ (v)
1. tra cứu, tham khảo, hỏi (ý kiến) (to get information or advice from a person, book, etc.)
2. bàn bạc, trao đổi ý kiến, hội ý (to discuss something with someone before you make a decision)
 1
  • If you have any problems, consult the FAQs on our website.
  • If the symptoms get worse, consult your doctor.
  • If any of these symptoms occur while you are taking the medicine, consult your doctor immediately.
  • If you don’t know the meaning of a word, consult a dictionary.
  • Have you consulted your lawyer about this?


    • 2.
  • You shouldn't have done it without consulting me.
  • Why didn't you consult me about this?
  • (n) consultant: người được hỏi ý kiến, cố vấn, tư vấn viên
  • a consultant
  • (n) consultancy:
    1. sự tư vấn     (the activity of giving advice on a particular subject)
    2. cơ quan, văn phòng tư vấn (a company that gives advice on a particular subject)
  • a consultancy
