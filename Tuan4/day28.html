Từ / Cụm từ Phiên âm Nghĩa Ví dụ
brochure /broʊˈʃʊr/ (n) quảng cáo mỏng (a small magazine or book containing pictures and information about something or advertising something)
  • Could you send me a brochure?
  • The brochure will be ready for publication in September.
  • The brochure shows the products that are currently available.
  • The computer's features are detailed in our brochure.
    		•
    agenda /əˈdʒɛn.də/ (n) chương trình làm việc (a list of items to be discussed at a meeting)
  • Let's move on, or we will not have time to cover everything on the agenda.
  • Let's skip to the last item on the agenda.
  • What's the next item on the agenda?
  • Let's go on to the next item on the agenda.
    • YouTube: PEWPEW
    curriculum /kəˈrɪ.kyə.ləm/ (n) chương trình giảng dạy (the subjects that are included in a course of study or taught in a school, college, etc.)
  • Is German on your school's curriculum?
  • English is the one compulsory foreign language on the school curriculum.
  • (n, UK) curriculum vitae (CV): hồ sơ ứng tuyển
  • (n, US) résumé: hồ sơ ứng tuyển (a short written description of your education, qualifications, previous jobs, and sometimes also your personal interests, that you send to an employer when you are trying to get a job)
  • She sent her résumé to 50 companies, but didn't even get an interview.
    • YouTube: Eat Sleep Dream English
    itinerary /aɪˈtɪ.nəˌrɛ.ri/ (n) lộ trình (của chuyến đi) (a detailed plan of a trip, including the route and the places that you visit)
  • In order to be on time for their event, each Olympic athlete consulted their itinerary to determine the location and time for their event.
  • If you go backpacking through Europe, it is helpful to have a detailed itinerary with you so that you have time to visit every place you want to go.
  • I'll mail you a copy of my itinerary so you'll know where to reach me.
    		•
    journey /ˈdʒər.ni/ (n) cuộc hành trình (the act of travelling from one place to another, especially in a vehicle)
  • Have a safe journey!
  • We did the journey to Wales in five hours.
  • The journey was quite quick because the road was clear.
  • We broke our journey (= stopped for a short time) in St. Louis.
    		•
    voyage /ˈvɔɪ.ɪdʒ/ (n) hành trình dài trên biển (a long journey, especially by ship)
    (v) đi du lịch bằng đường biển
  • In their little boat they planned to voyage to distant lands.
  • Christopher Columbus brought cattle on his second voyage to America in 1493.
  • This was my first trip on the ocean and my first voyage in a steamboat.
    		•
