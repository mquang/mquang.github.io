hundred /ˈhʌn.drəd/ (n)
1. 100, bội số của 100

2. rất nhiều, rất đông, hàng trăm (a very large number of things or people)
 1.
  • I agree with you a hundred percent.
  • I make nine hundred dollars a week.
  • Over two hundred people attended the funeral.
  • Few people live beyond the age of a hundred.

    • 2.
  • There were hundreds of people at the pool today.
  • Tom has hundreds of .
  • (n) thousand:
    + 1000, bội số của 1000

    + rất nhiều, rất đông, hàng nghìn
  • The population of the village is about three thousand.
  • Your essay should be no more than two thousand words long.
  • I have a thousand things to do before we go away.
  • (n) million:
    + 1.000.000, bội số của 1.000.000

    + rất nhiều, rất đông, hàng triệu
  • The book sold more than a million copies.
  • The event must have cost millions.
  • (n) millionaire: triệu phú (a person who has money, property, etc. that is worth at least 1.000.000 dollars, pounds, euros, etc.)
  • You want me to buy you a new car - do you think I'm a millionaire or something?
  • (n) billion: tỷ, hàng tỷ
  • There are billions of stars in the sky.
  • (n) billionaire: tỷ phú
  • The new mayor is a billionaire with no prior government experience.
  • (n) trillion: nghìn tỷ (1,000,000,000,000), hàng nghìn tỷ
  • We are targeting the $6.7 trillion market in imports and exports.
