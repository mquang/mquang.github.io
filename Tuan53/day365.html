  • Is your salary keeping up with inflation?
  • Inflation has made it impossible for many families to buy food without government aid.

    slate /sleɪt/ (v) (bị động) được kì vọng, dự kiến (to be expected to happen in the future or to be expected to be or do something in the future)

  • The new store is slated to open in spring.
  • The is slated for next Thursday.

    thus /ðʌs/ (adv)

    2. do đó (as a result of something just mentioned)
    		 1.

  • ‘The most beautiful village in England.’ Thus did the artist William Morris describe the village of Bibury.

    • 2.
    (syn) hence, therefore
  • We do not own the building. Thus, it would be impossible for us to make any major changes to it.
  • She did well in high school and thus will get into a good college.
  • He got caught cheating on his test and thus will be punished by the teacher.
  • I failed the class and thus I will have to take it again next semester.
  • Most of the evidence was destroyed in the fire. Thus it would be almost impossible to prove him guilty.

    bulk /bʌlk/ (n) số lượng lớn, sự to lớn (the large size or quantity of something)

  • Wholesalers who order in bulk will receive an additional 15% discount.

  • The bulk of our employees are recruited from overseas.
