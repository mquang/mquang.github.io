  • They exchanged hellos and forced smiles.
  • (v) force:
    (v) bắt ép, bắt buộc, cưỡng buộc, đành phải     (to make somebody do something that they do not want to do)

    (n)
    1. sức mạnh, sức (gió...)     (physical, often violent, strength or power)

    2. lực lượng (quân đội, công an...) (a group of people who have been trained to do military work for a government or other organization)
    • (v)
    - force somebody into doing something
    - force somebody/yourself to do something
  • I was forced to take a taxi because the last bus had left.
  • As he had no job and no savings, Alfred was forced to sell his car in order to keep paying the rent on his apartment.
  • The President was forced to resign.
  • The President was forced into resigning.

    • (n1)
  • The force of the wind knocked down many trees during the hurricane.
  • She had to use force to get the old window open.

  • the security forces

  • my ignorance, but how does it actually work?
  • (adj) ignorant: dốt, thiếu kiến thức, thiếu hiểu biết (lacking knowledge or information about something; not educated)

  • Many of the details of the events leading up to the bombing have come to light in recent months.

    perception /pəˈsep.ʃən/ (n) cái nhìn, cảm nghĩ, cảm nhận
    - what people think of something according to their views and what they see
    - the way that someone thinks and feels about a company, product, service, etc.
  • People's perception of Jake was very positive, as he was always smiling and helping others.
  • These photographs will affect people's perceptions of war.
  • After he took over, he changed public perceptions (=what people thought) of the company.
  • (adj) perceptive: tinh ý, nhạy bén
    + able to notice and understand things that many other people do not notice
    + having or showing the ability to see or understand things quickly, especially things that are not obvious
  • Her books are full of perceptive insights.
  • It was very perceptive of you to notice that.
    		•

    - to put a computer or piece of electronic equipment inside a product in order to control the way it operates
