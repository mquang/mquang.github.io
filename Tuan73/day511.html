Từ / Cụm từ Phiên âm Nghĩa Ví dụ
acronym /ˈæk.rə.nɪm/ (n) từ được cấu tạo bởi chữ cái đầu của những từ khác, từ viết tắt (a word made up from the first letters of the name of something such as an organization)
  • NASA is an acronym for National Aeronautics and Space Administration.
  • NATO is an acronym for the North Atlantic Treaty Organization.
    		•
    abbreviation /əˌbriː.viˈeɪ.ʃən/ (n) dạng viết gọn, viết tắt (a short form of a word or phrase)
  • Ad lib is an abbreviation of the Latin phrase ‘ad libitum’.
  • ‘Dr’ is the written abbreviation of ‘Doctor’.
    		•
    pamper /ˈpæm.pɚ/ (v) nuông chiều, chiều chuộng (to give someone special treatment, making that person as comfortable as possible and giving them whatever they want)
  • Pamper yourself with our new range of beauty treatments.
  • Pamper yourself with a stay in one of our luxury hotels.
  • She really pampers that dog - it's even got its own little bed next to hers.
    		•
    fuck off (ph.v) biến đi, cút mm đi (used especially as a rude way of telling someone to go away)
  • Just fuck off and leave me alone!
    		•
    hyped up /ˌhaɪpt ˈʌp/ (adj) (too excited or nervous and unable to rest or be calm)
  • We were both so hyped up about buying the house, we could not wait to get in there.
  • She gets really hyped up about job interviews.
    		•
    nonsense /ˈnɑːn.sens/ (n) (điều/lí lẽ/ý tưởng) ngu xuẩn
  • The accusations are nonsense.
  • How can you believe such nonsense?
    		•
    genre /ˈʒɑːn.rə/ (n) thể loại (a particular type or style of literature, art, film or music that you can recognize because of its special features)
  • What genre does the book fall into - comedy or tragedy?
  • He has explored a range of musical genres.
    		•
    blockbuster /ˈblɑːkˌbʌs.tɚ/ (n) bộ phim, tác phẩm (thành công) (a book or film that is very successful)
