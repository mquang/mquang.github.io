vacant /ˈveɪ.kənt/ (adj)
1. trống; chưa có ai dùng, sử dụng (not filled or occupied; available to be used)


2. (công việc/vị trí) trống, chưa có ai làm (if a job in a company is vacant, nobody is doing it and it is available for somebody to take)		 1.
  • We have three vacant apartments in our building.
  • Is this seat vacant?
  • She parked her car in a vacant lot.
  • Do you have any vacant rooms?

    • 2.
  • The position fell (= became) vacant when Rodman was promoted.
  • The office of president had been left vacant since her retirement.
