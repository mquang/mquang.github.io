tell /tel/ (v)
1. biết được, nhận biết được, cảm nhận được (to know, recognize, or be certain)

2. phân biệt được (to be able to see how one person or thing is different from another)		 1.
  • "He's Dutch." "How can you tell?"

  • The moment Kramer walked in, I could tell that things were not going well.

  • How can you tell a fake Vuitton handbag from the real thing?
