suicide /ˈsuː.ə.saɪd/ (n) tự sát (the act of killing yourself intentionally)
  • Many suicides occur in prisons.
  • All the evidence points to suicide.
  • She had attempted suicide twice.
  • (adj) suicidal:
    1. people who are suicidal feel that they want to kill themselves

    2. very dangerous and likely to lead to death; likely to cause very serious problems or disaster
    • 1.
  • For many years before treatment, Clare had suicidal tendencies (=behaviour that showed she wanted to kill herself).
  • Pete was so depressed after his girlfriend left him that I actually thought he was suicidal.
  • On bad days I even felt suicidal.

    • 2.
  • It would be suicidal to risk going out in this weather.
  • Her economic policies would prove suicidal for our economy.
