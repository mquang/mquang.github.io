rational /ˈræʃənəl/ (adj) dựa trên lý lẽ
(of thoughts, decisions, ideas, etc.) based on reason rather than emotions
  • We need to present them with a rational argument.
  • Let's all calm down and have a rational discussion.
  • Although the arguments were rational, he was not convinced.
  • (adj, opp) irrational: (not based on, or not using, clear logical thought)
  • I knew I was being irrational, but when she was late I began to fear the worst.
  • His parents were worried by his increasingly irrational behaviour.
