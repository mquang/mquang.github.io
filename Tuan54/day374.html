touchy /ˈtʌtʃi/ (adj) nhạy cảm, dễ bị đụng chạm, tự ái
  • (of a person) easily upset or offended
  • (of a subject) that may upset or offend people and should therefore be dealt with carefully

    		•
    (syn) sensitive
  • He's very touchy about the fact that his wife was married before.
  • Why are you so touchy today?
  • This is a touchy subject, so we'd better avoid it.
    		•

    (v) moi ruột (to remove the inner organs of an animal, especially in preparation for eating it)

  • It can take up to 72 hours for food to pass through the gut.

    • (n2)

    homogenous /hoʊˈmɑːdʒə.nəs/ (adj) giống nhau, tương đồng nhau (consisting of parts or people that are similar to each other or are of the same type)
    also homogeneous

  • At my school, the smartest students are placed in homogenous classes so they can challenge each other.
