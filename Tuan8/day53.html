Từ / Cụm từ Phiên âm Nghĩa Ví dụ
immediately /ɪˈmiː.di.ət.li/ (adv) ngay, ngay lập tức, ngay khi, ở ngay
- without delay
- next to or very close to a particular place
  • I want you to stop this immediately.
  • She recognized him immediately.
  • The telephone rang, and he answered it immediately.
  • Dinner was served immediately after the ceremony.
  • Turn right immediately after the church.
  • Turn left, and the bathroom is immediately on the right.


  • (synonym) at once:
    1. immediately
    2. cùng 1 lúc     (at the same time)
    • 1.
  • Come here at once!
  • He came home at once when he heard his wife was ill.

    • 2.
  • I can't do two things at once.
  • Everything happened at once – she graduated, got a job, and got married, all in June!
    		•
    instant /ˈɪn.stənt/ (adj)
    1. ngay lập tức, ngay tức khắc
    (happening immediately, without any delay)
    2. ăn/uống liền
    (of food) that can be made quickly and easily, usually by adding hot water
    (n) ngay (a very short period of time)    		 (adj)
    synonym: immediate

    1.
  • a system that provides instant access to client information

    • 2.
  • instant coffee/soup/noodles

    • (n)
  • "Stop that noise this instant (= now)!"
  • I'll call you the instant (= as soon as) I get home.
  • I'll be back in an instant.
  • I recognized her the instant (that)(= as soon as) I saw her.
  • A microwave oven gets food hot in an instant.
  • (adv) instantly: (syn) immediately
  • Tom was killed instantly when the bomb he was trying to defuse exploded.
  • The driver was killed instantly.
  • Tom instantly recognized Mary's voice.
  • With e-mail you can send a message across the world almost instantly.
    		•
    urgent /ˈɝː.dʒənt/ (adj) gấp, cấp bách (quan trọng)
    (very important and needing to be dealt with immediately)
  • Your sister's been calling -- I think it's urgent.
  • ‘Can I see you for a moment?’ - ‘Is it urgent?’
  • I canceled my appointment because of urgent business.
  • Excuse me, but I have some urgent business to attend to.
  • If there's anything urgent, you can get in touch with me.
  • I need to make an urgent call. Is there a public phone near here?
    		•
    emergency /ɪˈmɝː.dʒən.si/ (n) trường hợp/tình trạng khẩn cấp, cấp bách (cấp cứu)
    (adj) khẩn cấp, cấp bách
    		 (n)
  • In an emergency, dial 911 for an ambulance.
  • How would disabled people escape in an emergency?
  • I always have some extra cash with me for emergencies.
  • Lifeguards are trained to deal with emergencies.
  • In case of emergency, press the alarm button.
    • (adj)
  • Seven board members called for an emergency meeting.
    		•
    promptly /ˈprɑːmptli/ (adv)
    1. nhanh chóng, mau lẹ
    2. đúng (giờ)     (exactly at the correct time, on time)    		 1.
  • She turned off the alarm and promptly went back to sleep.
  • She deals with all the correspondence promptly and efficiently.
  • She repeated the Vietnamese words I taught her, but promptly forgot them again.

    • 2.
  • She arrived promptly at 9 o’clock.
  • They arrived promptly at two o'clock.
  • Promptly at a quarter past seven, we left the hotel.

  • (adj, v) prompt:
    (adj) nhanh chóng; đúng giờ
    (of an action) done quickly and without delay, or (of a person) acting quickly, arriving at the right time
    (v) xúi dục, thúc đẩy, khiến, nhắc, gợi ý
    - to make something happen
    - to make someone decide to say or do something
    (n) a sign that appears on a computer screen showing that the the computer has finished one operation and is ready to begin the next
    • (adj)
  • Thank you for your prompt reply.
  • We guarantee prompt delivery of your purchases.
  • Please be prompt when attending these meetings.
  • Lunch is at two. Try to be prompt.

    • (v)
  • What prompted you to ?
  • I don't know what prompted him to leave.
  • The computer prompted me to type in a number.

    • (n)
  • When you see the "C" prompt, type "WP."
    		•
