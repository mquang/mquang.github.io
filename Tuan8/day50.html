prominent /ˈprɑː.mə.nənt/ (adj)
1. nổi tiếng, đóng 1 vai trò quan trọng (very well known and important, FAMOUS)

2. (địa điểm, vị trí) nổi bật, dễ thấy

3. nhô ra, nhô lên, lồi lên		 1.
  • a prominent politician
  • She was prominent in the fashion industry.
  • She plays a prominent role in the organization.
  • He played a prominent part in the campaign.
  • Many of Hollywood’s prominent actors will be at the president’s fundraiser tomorrow.

    • 2.
  • The shop front occupies a very prominent position on the main street.
  • The story was given a prominent position on the front page.
  • Her photo stood in a prominent place on his desk.
  • New books are displayed in a prominent position on tables at the front of the store.

    • 3.
  • She has a prominent chin/nose.
  • prominent cheekbones
    		•
    outstanding /ˌaʊtˈstæn.dɪŋ/ (adj)
    1. xuất sắc, tuyệt vời, nổi bật, ưu tú (EXCELLENT, extremely good, very much better than usual)


    2. còn tồn tại, chưa giải quyết xong, chưa thanh toán (not yet paid, done, solved, etc.)    		 1.
  • an outstanding performance/novel
  • an outstanding
  • She is an outstanding .
  • Outstanding among the menu items were the tomato soup and the swordfish.
  • The painting is an outstanding example of the artist's style.
  • an outstanding example of Indian art

    • 2.
  • She has outstanding debts of over £500.
  • A lot of work is still outstanding.
  • I have a lot of work still outstanding.
  • We still have some outstanding issues to discuss.
    		•
    remarkable /rɪˈmɑːr.kə.bəl/ (adj) đáng chú ý, xuất sắc (unusual or surprising and therefore deserving attention or praise)
  • Meeting you here in Rome is a remarkable coincidence.
  • Tom and Mary have achieved remarkable results using their new technique.
  • Competing in the Olympics is a remarkable achievement.
  • What a remarkable performance!
  • He has a remarkable memory.
  • Tom is remarkable.
  • Nelson Mandela was a truly remarkable man.
  • (n, v) remark:
    (n) lời nhận xét, bình luận     (something that you say or write which expresses an opinion, a thought, etc. about somebody/something)

    (v) nhận xét, bình luận (to say or write a comment about something/somebody)
    (syn) COMMENT
    • (n)
  • He made a number of rude remarks about the food.

    • (v)
  • She remarked how tired I was looking.
  • He remarked that she was looking thin.
  • All his friends remarked on the change in him.
    		•
    highlight /ˈhaɪ.laɪt/ (n) điểm nổi bật, nét nổi bật, sự kiện nổi bật
    (v) làm nổi bật
    		 (n)
  • Highlights of the match will be shown after the news.
  • I missed the game, but I saw the highlights on the evening news.
  • At 11.30 we’ll be showing highlights from the third round of the FA Cup.
  • The weekend in Venice was definitely the highlight of our trip.
  • The highlight of our trip was a stay at the Inn On Tomales Bay.

    • (v)
  • The spelling mistakes in the text had been highlighted in green.
  • I've highlighted the important passages in yellow.
  • Your résumé should highlight your skills and achievements.
    		•
    significant /sɪɡˈnɪ.fə.kənt/ (adj) quan trọng, đáng kể (large or important enough to have an effect or to be noticed)
  • Please inform us if there are any significant changes in your plans.
  • There has been a significant increase in the number of women students in recent years.
  • Is there any significant difference in quality between these two items?
  • The difference in our ages is not significant.
  • I don't think there's any significant damage.
  • I don't see any significant difference.


  • (n) significance:
    1. sự quan trọng, tầm quan trọng     (the importance of an event, action etc, especially because of the effects or influence it will have in the future)

    2. ý nghĩa
    + the meaning of a word, sign, action etc, especially when this is not immediately clear
    + special meaning
    • 1.
  • the significance of climate change.
  • The book assesses the significance of Stalin’s policies between 1927 and 1939.

    • 2.
  • Do you think that look he gave you had any significance?
  • Does the date March 16 have some special significance to you?
  • This building should be preserved because of its historical significance.
