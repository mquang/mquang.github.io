Từ / Cụm từ Phiên âm Nghĩa Ví dụ
suitable /ˈsuː.t̬ə.bəl/ (adj) phù hợp
  • We are hoping to find a suitable school.
  • I was looking for a birthday present for my mother but I didn't find anything suitable.
    • suitable to/for
  • The film is not suitable for children.
  • Each room has books, toys, and games that are suitable to that particular age group.
  • The programme is suitable for children of all ages.
  • (n) suitability: sự phù hợp, thích hợp
  • There were doubts about his suitability for the job.
  • Tests were done to determine her suitability for a transplant.
  • Maybe I was hasty about Roy and Hayley's suitability to adopt.
  • assessments of their suitability to adopt a child
  • (n) suit: bộ com lê (a jacket and trousers or a jacket and skirt that are made from the same material)
    • The Incredibles (2004)
    accordingly /əˈkɔːr.dɪŋ.li/ (adv)
    1. một cách phù hợp, thích đáng (in a way that is suitable or right for the situation)

    2. do đó (as a result of something - therefore, consequently)    		 1.
  • They have broken the rules and will be punished accordingly.
  • Read the instructions and act accordingly.
  • He knew his limitations and acted accordingly.

    • 2.
  • There aren't many jobs available. Accordingly, companies receive hundreds of resumés for every opening.
    		•
    fit /fɪt/ (v)
    1. vừa

    2. phù hợp, khớp
    (adj)
    1. phù hợp

    2. khỏe mạnh (healthy and strong, especially as a result of exercise)
    		 (v1)
  • That jacket fits you perfectly.
  • Our new sofa doesn't fit through the door.
  • Those trousers fit you nicely.
  • The jacket’s fine, but the trousers don’t fit.
  • I wanted to put the wardrobe behind the door, but I don’t think it’ll fit.
    • (phrase) fit like a glove: vừa khít lỗ đ** =))
    (to be the perfect size and shape for someone)
  • I love these pants because they fit like a glove and they're so comfortable.
  • My new shoes fit like a glove.


    • (v2)
  • I'm sure we'll have something to fit your requirements.
  • He fitted his schedule to mine.
  • Police said the car fits the description of the stolen vehicle.
    • (adj1)
  • He is not fit to govern this country!
  • The food was not fit for human consumption.

    • (adj2)
  • I swim twice a week to try and keep fit.
  • She tries to keep fit by jogging every day.
    • fitness
    adaptable /əˈdæp.tə.bəl/ (adj) có thể thích ứng, thích nghi (nhiều hoàn cảnh)
  • I'm not sure Ken's adaptable enough to take a job abroad.
  • Children are often more adaptable than adults.
  • We need adaptable workers who are willing to learn new skills.
  • (v) adapt: sửa lại cho phù hợp, thích ứng, thích nghi
  • We had to adapt our plans to fit Jack's timetable.
  • Tom adapted last year's lesson plans so he could use them with this year's lower level students.
  • It took me a while to adapt to the new job.
  • When children go to a different school, it usually takes them a while to adapt.
  • (n) adapter: dây sạc, dây nối nguồn
    - a type of plug that makes it possible to connect two or more pieces of equipment to the same electrical supply
    - a device that is used to connect two pieces of equipment
    (là thiết bị chuyển đổi điện áp 220V xuống một điện áp thấp hơn để phù hợp với các thiết bị điện tử => khớp với nghĩa làm thích ứng, thích nghi)
