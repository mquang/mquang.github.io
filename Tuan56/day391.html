    at large (adv) nhìn chung, nói chung, hầu hết (generally, in general; most people, rather than just some of them)

    (adj) nhởn nhơ ngoài vòng pháp luật (if a dangerous person or animal is at large, they have escaped from somewhere or have not been caught)
    		 (adv)
  • That change in curriculum has not been accepted by the teachers at large.
  • Truck drivers at large don't like the new speed restriction on the highway.

    • (adj)
  • Two of the escaped prisoners are still at large.

    go-to /ˈɡoʊˌtuː/ (adj) (the go-to person, organization, website etc is the best one to use when you need to achieve something)
    (n) (the best person, thing, or place for a particular purpose or need)
    		 (adj)
  • For 20 years, Wild Mountain was the go-to store for outdoor enthusiasts.
  • He was everyone's go-to guy for computer problems.
  • TikTok has become a go-to place for record labels to scout new talent.

    • (n)
  • Your site is always a go-to for when I need ideas.
