qualifier /ˈkwɑː.lə.faɪ.ɚ/ (n)
1. vòng loại (a game from which the winner will go on to compete in the next part of a competition)

2. từ hạn định (a word or phrase that limits or adds to the meaning of another word or phrase)
 1.
  • a World Cup qualifier
  • Belgium and Italy are playing in tonight's qualifier.

    • 2.
  • In ‘the open door’, ‘open’ is a qualifier, describing the door.
  • In the noun group ‘the rules of the game’, the prepositional phrase ‘of the game’ is a qualifier.
  • Qualifiers of quantity: some, most, all, none, etc.
  • Qualifiers of time: occasionally, sometimes, now and again, usually, always, never, etc.
  • There are two types of qualifiers available in C language. They are const, volatile.
