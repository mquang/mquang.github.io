Từ / Cụm từ Phiên âm Nghĩa Ví dụ
dispense /dɪˈspɛns/ (v)
1. cung cấp, phân phát, phân phối (to provide something such as a service, money, or information)

2. cấp thuốc (to prepare medicines as a job and give or sell them to people)
  • The machine dispenses a variety of drinks and snacks.
  • The company makes vending machines that dispense drinks and snacks.
  • The Red Cross dispensed food and medical supplies to the victims.
  • Pharmacists are certified to dispense medication.
  • (phrasal verb) dispense with somebody/something: bỏ qua, bỏ (to stop using something or someone that you do not need)
  • I think we can dispense with the formalities (= speak openly and naturally to each other).
  • We can dispense with any formalities, Ms. Correa.
  • Let's dispense with formalities.
  • They've had to dispense with a lot of luxuries since Mike lost his job.
  • (n) dispensary: quầy cấp thuốc (a place where medicines are prepared and given out, often in a hospital)
  • (n) dispenser: a machine or container that you can get something from
  • a soap dispenser

  • a tape dispenser

  • water dispenser



  • (adj) dispensable: thừa thãi
  • (adj) indispensable: không thể thiếu
  • This book is an indispensable resource for researchers.
  • To most teenagers, their mobile phones are indispensable to their happiness.
    		•
    deliver /dɪˈlɪvər/ (v) phân phối, giao (hàng)
  • "What would be the best time of day for us to deliver the table?"
    "Oh, any time will be OK."
  • I will deliver it to you.
  • The furniture store is delivering our new bed on Thursday.
  • Mail is delivered to our office twice a day.
  • (n) delivery: sự phân phối, giao hàng
  • You can pay for the carpet on delivery (= when it is delivered).
  • Is there mail delivery on Saturdays?
  • When can you take delivery of (= be available to receive) the car?
    		•
    allocate /ˈæ.ləˌkeɪt/ (v) cấp (tiền, nguồn lực,...); dùng (tiền, nguồn lực,...) vào việc gì (to give a particular amount of time, money, etc. to someone or something, so that it can be used in a particular way)
  • They allocated 3% of the advertising budget to newspaper ads.
  • A large sum has been allocated for buying new books for the library.
  • More resources are being allocated to the project.
  • The government is allocating £10 million for health education.
  • As project leader, you will have to allocate jobs to people.
    		•
    distribute /dɪˈstrɪ.byut/ (v) phân phối, phân phát
  • The Red Cross distributed food to the refugees.
  • Food and clothing are being distributed to the flood victims.
  • They will distribute the at the meeting.
  • (n) distribution: sự phân phối, phân phát, phân bổ (dân số)
  • He bought a map showing the population distribution of Scotland.
  • The corresponding value for the distribution of iodine in benzene and water is about 400.
  • (n) distributor: phân phối, phân phát (a person or company that supplies goods to stores, etc.)
  • You can get the book through your local distributor.
    		•
    attribute /əˈtrɪ.byut/ (n) thuộc tính (a quality or characteristic that someone or something has)
    (v) cho là (to say or think that something is the result of a particular thing)
  • Patience is an essential attribute for a teacher.
  • Organizational ability is an essential attribute for a good manager.
  • (phrasal verb) attribute something to something: cho rằng ... là ...
  • She attributes her success to hard work and a little luck.
  • I attribute our success to him.
  • Her success can be attributed to three main factors.
    • attribute cũng được nhắc đến trong lập trình hướng đối tượng
    contribute /kənˈtrɪ.byut/ (v) đóng góp, góp phần (to give something, especially money, in order to provide or achieve something together with other people)
  • Do you contribute to charity?
  • Tourism contributes millions of pounds to the country's economy.
  • My roommate and I contribute equally to the household budget.
  • Everyone in the office is contributing money for his leaving present.
  • (n) contributor: người đóng góp, người cộng tác (soạn báo...)
  • (n) contribution: sự đóng góp
  • Marie Curie is famous for her contribution to science.
  • They make contributions to society.
  • Einstein was awarded the Nobel Prize for his contribution to Quantum Theory.
    		•
