complete /kəmˈplit/ (adj)
1. hoàn toàn
2. hoàn thành, xong
(v) hoàn thành, hoàn tất		 1.
  • We are in complete agreement.
  • She made a complete change in her appearance.

    • 2.
  • The project is complete.
  • Her painting is almost complete.

    • 3.
  • Did you complete the work?
  • Can you complete the job in two days?

  • (adv) completely: hoàn toàn (bổ nghĩa cho động từ)
  • I agree with you completely.
  • He'd completely changed - I didn't recognize him.

    finish /ˈfɪ.nɪʃ/ (v)
    1. hoàn thành (to complete something or come to the end of an activity)
    2. kết thúc (to end)
  • I'll call you when I've finished my homework.
  • She finished (the concert) with a song from her first album.
  • Have you finished reading that magazine?
  • School starts at 9 a.m. and finishes at 3.30 p.m.

    		•
    achieve /əˈtʃiv/ (v) đạt được (to succeed in finishing something or reaching an aim, especially after a lot of work or effort)
  • They achieved high scores on their math tests.
  • You'll never achieve anything if you don't study harder.
  • (n) achievement: thành tích, thành tựu
  • His career as a journalist was full of distinguished achievements.
  • Obtaining my degree was a big achievement for me.
  • They were proud of their children's achievements.
    		•
    archive /ˈɑr.kaɪv/ (n)
    1. tài liệu lưu trữ (a collection of historical records relating to a place, organization, or family)
    2. di tích (a place where historical records are kept)
    (v) lưu trữ
  • These old photographs should go in the family archives.
  • Students took a trip to the historical archive to find primary documents for their research papers.
  • This software helps firms archive and retrieve emails.
    		•
    accomplish /əˈkɑm.plɪʃ/ (v) hoàn thành, làm xong
  • That's it. Mission accomplished.
  • The students accomplished the task in less than ten minutes.
  • (n) accomplishment:
    1. sự hoàn thành     (the finishing of something)
    2. thành tích, thành tựu (an impressive thing that is done or achieved after a lot of work)
    (synonym: achievement)
    • 1
  • We celebrated the successful accomplishment of our task.

    • 2
  • Being the first one in my family to graduate from college is an accomplishment that I’m very proud of.
  • That was the greatest accomplishment of my life.
  • (adj) accomplished: tài ba, có chuyên môn cao (very good at a particular thing; having a lot of skills)
