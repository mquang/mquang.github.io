Từ / Cụm từ Phiên âm Nghĩa Ví dụ
attain /əˈteɪn/ (v) đạt được (to succeed in getting something, usually after a lot of effort)
synonym: achieve
  • India attained independence in 1947, after decades of struggle.
  • Anyone can attain success with determination and hard work.
  • This kind of tree can attain a height of 20 feet within just a few years.
  • The car can attain a top speed of 200 mph.
    		•
    obtain /əbˈteɪn/ (v)
    1. đạt được, thu được (to get something, especially by asking for it, buying it, working for it, or producing it from something else)


    2. đang tồn tại, hiện hành (of rules, systems, customs, etc.) to exist    		 1.
  • In the second experiment they obtained a very clear result.
  • Sugar is obtained by crushing and processing sugar cane.
  • I will have obtained a driver's license by the end of the year.

    • 2.
  • These conditions no longer obtain.
    • Phân biệt attain và obtain:
    + attain: đạt được thành tựu, thành tích (achievement)
    + obtain: đạt được quyền sở hữu (possession) của cái gì


  • Do you think that car can attain a speed of 100 miles per hour?
  • Jessica obtained a book by borrowing Jeffrey's.
  • Police obtained a warrant to search the house.
  • The robbers obtained weapons to commit the crime.
    		•
    gain /ɡeɪn/ (v)
    1. thu được, đạt được
    2. tăng

    (n)
    1. lợi, lợi ích, lợi lộc     (something useful or good that you get for yourself)
    2. sự tăng (an increase in size, weight, amount, etc.)
    (v)
  • What do you hope to gain from the course?
  • The country gained its independence ten years ago.
  • She gained membership in the organization.
    • gain weight: tăng cân
  • I gained a lot of weight while I was on holiday.
  • The shares gained 14 cents.

    • (n)
  • The company achieved huge gains this year.
  • Lack of exercise can cause weight gain.
    		•
    acquire /əˈkwaɪ.ər/ (v) đạt được, giành được, thu được, mua được (to obtain or begin to have something)
    		 Silicon Valley
  • How long will it take to acquire the necessary skills?
  • (n) acquisition:
    1. sự giành được, sự thu được, sự thu nạp, sự mua
    + the process of getting something
    + the act of getting something, especially knowledge, a skill, etc.
    2. vật (thứ) thu được, thu nhận được (a company, piece of land, etc. bought by someone, especially another company; the act of buying it)
  • Language acquisition (= learning a language without being taught) starts at a very young age.
  • Mr. A will be a valuable acquisition to the teaching staff of our school.
    		•
    inquire /ɪnˈkwaɪər/ (v) hỏi, hỏi thăm (to ask for information)
    inquire = enquire (đồng nghĩa + đồng âm)
  • I inquired about the book in many stores.
  • Shall I inquire about the price of tickets?
  • (phrasal verb) inquire after sb: hỏi thăm (to ask for information about someone, especially about their health or about what they are doing, in order to be polite)
  • She inquired after his grandfather's health.
  • I went to the hospital to inquire after his health.
  • (phrasal verb) inquire into something: tìm hiểu, tìm ra sự thật (to try to discover the facts about something)
  • The police are now inquiring into the cause of the fire.
  • The committee had no right to inquire into his politics.
  • (n) inquiry:
    1. sự điều tra     (an official process to find out the cause of something or to find out information about something)
    2. câu hỏi (a question or request for information about someone or something)
  • She refused to answer inquiries from the media about her marriage.
    		•
