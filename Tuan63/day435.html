    1. sự hấp dẫn, sự quyến rũ, cám dỗ

    sentiment /ˈsen.tɪ.mənt/ (n) tình cảm, cảm xúc (chi phối công việc) (feelings of sympathy, romantic love, sadness, etc. that are often considered to be too strong or not suitable for a particular situation)
  • There’s no for sentiment in business!
  • (adj) sentimental:
    1. sến sẩm, thể hiện tình cảm quá đà     (having or expressing strong feelings of love, sadness, etc., in a way that seems foolish or excessive to other people)

    2. vì (lí do) tình cảm thay vì dựa trên lí lẽ, thực tế (A sentimental person is strongly influenced by emotional feelings, especially about happy memories of past events or relationships with other people, rather than by careful thought and judgment based on facts)
