Từ / Cụm từ Phiên âm Nghĩa Ví dụ
ancient /ˈeɪn.ʃənt/ (adj) xưa, cổ / cũ
  • The ancient Egyptians
  • The ancient Greeks
  • He's got an ancient laptop.
  • Ancient farming methods still survive in the Middle East.
  • Some ancient laws are still in effect.
  • (synonym) antique:
    (adj) cổ
    (n) đồ cổ
  • My grandfather collects antiques.
  • Tom spent all day looking around antique shops.
  • She has some beautiful antique furniture.
  • This antique clock is worth one thousand dollars.
  • Can you tell me where the nearest antique shop is?
  • (adj) antiquated: cũ kĩ, lỗi thời (old-fashioned or unsuitable for modern society)
  • antiquated technology
    		•
    accent /ˈæk.sent/ (n) giọng
  • He speaks with a strong southern accent.
  • He's always mocking my French accent.
  • You have a perfect English accent.
  • She's French but she speaks with an impeccable English accent.
    		•
    altitude /ˈæl.tɪ.tuːd/ (n)
    1. độ cao (so với mặt nước biển)

    2. (toán) đường cao    		 1.
  • We are flying at an altitude of 6000 metres.
  • We are currently flying at an altitude of 15000 metres.
  • What is the altitude of the top of the mountain?


    • 2.
  • Every triangle has three altitudes.

    		•
    attitude /ˈæ.t̬ɪ.tuːd/ (n) thái độ
  • I don't like your attitude (= the way you are behaving).
  • You have to change your attitude.
  • If you want to pass your exams you'd better change your attitude!
  • He has a very bad attitude to/towards work.
    		•
    attach /əˈtætʃ/ (v) đính kèm, gắn liền với
  • I attached a photo to my application form.
  • Don't forget to attach the label to your luggage.
    		•
    attack /əˈtæk/ (v)
  • tấn công
  • công kích (báo chí)
    (n)
    cơn (bệnh) - sốt rét, cúm, đau tim...
    		•
  • Her voice shook as she spoke about the person who attacked her.
  • Snakes will only attack if you disturb them.
  • She wrote an article attacking the judges and their conduct of the trial.
  • an attack of fever: một cơn sốt
  • a heart attack: một cơn đau tim
  • A third heart attack finally finished off the old man.
    		•
    comment /ˈkɑːment/ (n) lời bình luận
    (v) bình luận
  • I don't want any comments on/about my new haircut, thank you!
  • She made helpful comments on my work.
  • Please email us or leave a comment on our website.
  • (idiom) No comment: = I have nothing to say about that (dùng trong tình huống bị hỏi bởi phóng viên (chẳng hạn) và bạn không muốn trả lời)
    		•
    commend /kəˈmend/ (v)
    1. tuyên dương, tán dương, khen ngợi


    2. gửi gắm, giao phó, phó thác
  • The judge commended her for/on her bravery.
  • His designs were highly commended by the judges.
    • commend somebody/something to somebody
  • We commend her soul to God.
  • Tuan, I commend this report to you.

    • + commend somebody (for something/for doing something)
    + commend somebody (on something/on doing something)
    (khen ngợi ai về việc)
  • She was commended on her handling of the situation.
  • (n) commendation: sự khen ngợi, sự tán dương
  • Several of the firefighters received commendation for their bravery.
    		•
    commence /kəˈmens/ (v) bắt đầu
    commence + V-ing
  • We will commence building work in August of next year.
  • Shall we let the meeting commence?
  • He commenced speaking before all the guests had arrived.
  • She commenced her medical career in 1956.


  • (n) commencement:
    1. sự bắt đầu (cái gì)

    2. lễ phát bằng
  • The commencement ceremony for those graduating from the nursing program will begin at 3 pm.
    		•
