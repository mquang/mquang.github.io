(v) ước tính, ước lượng
  • The official estimate is one million dollars.
  • We estimate the cost to be four thousand dollars.
  • I estimate his age at thirty - five.
  • Can you estimate ?

    (sự) đánh giá quá cao (hơn những gì nó có); ước tính, ước lượng quá mức (hơn những gì thực sự cần)     (to think that something is larger or greater than it really is)
  • I overestimated and there was a lot of food left over after the party.
  • Most people overestimate what they can achieve in a day.

    exclusive /ɪkˈskluː.sɪv/ (adj)
    1. độc quyền, chỉ dành riêng cho (limited to only one person or group of people)


    2. trừ cái gì ra, không kể (not including something)    		 1.
  • From our website you can watch exclusive video clips and audio clips.
  • Many of their cheeses are exclusive to our stores in Britain.



    • 2.
    exclusive of sth: trừ cái gì ra, không kể đến
  • Is the total exclusive of service charges?
  • Skiing weekends cost £58 (exclusive of travel and accommodation).
  • The cost is £20 exclusive of delivery charges.
  • (n) exclusion: sự ngăn chặn, trừ (cái gì) ra
  • (v) exclude:
    1. ngăn chặn

    2. khai trừ, đuổi ra, tống ra

    3. trừ cái gì ra
  • Tom has been excluded from class for bad behaviour.
  • Women are still excluded from the club.
  • All the members of our club, excluding me, are men.
  • Try excluding fat from your diet.
