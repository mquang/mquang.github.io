Từ / Cụm từ Phiên âm Nghĩa Ví dụ
eligible /ˈe.lɪ.dʒə.bl/ (adj) đủ điều kiện, đủ tư cách, thích hợp
  • Who is eligible to apply for membership of the association?
  • Are you eligible to join this club?
  • Only people over 18 are eligible to vote.
    		•
    caution /ˈkɔːʃn/ (n) sự thận trọng, sự cẩn thận
  • You should drive with caution when it is foggy outside.
  • Caution was not her style.
  • (adj) cautious: thận trọng, cẩn thận
  • a cautious driver
  • a cautious approach
  • (n) precaution: sự phòng ngừa, đề phòng
    		•
    as well as (phrase) cũng như
  • Trees give us fruits as well as shade.
  • She owns a car as well as a motorbike.
  • Maya as well as her friends is studying for the test.
  • The cow as well as the goat gives us milk.
    • * tương đương not only ... but also: không những ... mà còn
  • She is clever as well as beautiful.
  • She is not only beautiful, but also clever.
  • Maria excels not only in mathematics but also in science.
  • Marta excels in mathematics as well as in science.
    		•
    influence /ˈɪn.flu.əns/ (n) ảnh hưởng
  • His first music teacher was a major influence in his life.
    • influence on somebody/something
  • What exactly is the influence of television on children?
  • Those friends are a bad influence on her.
    		•
    fluent /ˈfluːənt/ (adj) lưu loát, trôi chảy
  • She's fluent in Polish.
  • ‘Can he speak German?’ ‘Yes, he’s fluent.’
  • He is fluent in five languages.
  • He speaks fluent Japanese.
  • (n) fluency: sự lưu loát, trôi chảy
  • To improve your fluency, you should try speaking with native speakers as often as you can.
  • I'm amazed at your fluency in English.
    		•
    affordable /əˈfɔːr.də.bl/ (adj) (giá cả) phải chăng
  • The restaurant serves good food at affordable prices.
  • There are few affordable apartments in big cities.
  • (phrase) can/can't afford to do something: đủ/ không đủ tài chính để chi trả
  • We can't afford to go abroad this summer.
  • How can he afford to buy himself a brand new car?
  • How can she afford to eat out every night?
    • Song: Closer - The Chainsmokers ft. Halsey
    expense /ɪkˈspens/ (n) chi phí, phí tổn
  • I don't think we can afford the expense.
  • He traveled at his company's expense.
  • We went on holiday at my father's expense
  • Just buy it - never mind the expense!
  • (adj) expensive: đắt tiền, tốn kém nhiều tiền
  • Rolls Royces are very expensive.
  • Big houses are expensive to maintain.
    		•
    extend /ɪkˈstend/ (v) mở rộng, kéo dài
  • Can you extend your visit a few days longer?
  • Her visit will extend from Monday to Thursday.
  • The examinations extend over two weeks.
  • The company plans to extend its operations into Europe.
  • We have plans to extend our house.
  • (n) extension:
    1. việc nới rộng, gia hạn, cho thêm (thời gian)     (an extra, additional period of time allowed for something)

    2. số máy nhánh điện thoại (a subsidiary telephone in a set of offices or similar building, on a line leading from the main switchboard but having its own additional number)

    3. đuôi tệp tin (the last part of the name of a computer file, which comes after a (.) and shows what type of file it is)
    • 1.
  • I've applied for an extension to my visa.
  • She was given an extension to finish writing her thesis.

    • 2.
  • When you call, ask for extension 3276.
  • Can I have extension 316, please?
  • (adj) extensive: rộng, lớn, nhiều
  • Her knowledge of music is extensive (= she knows a lot about music).
  • The fire caused extensive damage.
