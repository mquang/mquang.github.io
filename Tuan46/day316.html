accessory /əkˈses.ər.i/ (n)
1. đồng phạm, đồng lõa, che giấu tội phạm (a person who helps somebody to commit a crime or who knows about it and protects the person from the police)

2. phụ kiện (something such as a piece of equipment or a decoration that is not necessary, but that makes a machine, car, room etc more useful or more attractive)
 1.
  • He was charged with being an accessory to murder.

    • 2.
  • The store specializes in wedding gowns and accessories.
  • The store also sells computer accessories, such as cables, batteries, and webcams.

  • Instead of completing your assignment, you come up with the absurd excuse that your dog ate your homework.
