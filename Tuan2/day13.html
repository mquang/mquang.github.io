Từ / Cụm từ Phiên âm Nghĩa Ví dụ
attempt /əˈtɛmpt/ (n, v) sự cố gắng, sự thử, sự ráng sức
  • He attempted to escape through a window.
  • He made an attempt at baking his first cake.
  • Don’t attempt to do these tricks at home.
  • I passed my driving test at the first attempt.

    • "Đang thử kết nối lại" trong LOL

    attend /əˈtɛnd/ (v) dự, có mặt
  • Over two hundred people attended the funeral.
  • The meeting is on the fifth and we're hoping everyone will attend.
  • You don’t have to attend if you don’t want to.
    • attend somebody: đi theo, hộ tống
    (syn: accompany)
  • The president was attended by several members of his staff.
  • The queen was attended by her ladies-in-waiting.
  • (syn/phrase) be present at: có mặt tại
  • I was present at/attended their wedding.
  • The whole family was present.
  • The mayor was present during the entire meeting.
  • (n) attendee: người tham dự
    		•
    intend /ɪnˈtɛnd/ (v) định, dự định, có ý định
    intend to do something (intend to + V)
  • We intend to go to Australia next year.
  • How do you intend to deal with this problem?
  • What do you intend to do ?

  • (n) intention: ý định, chủ ý
  • It wasn't my intention to exclude her from the list - I just forgot her.
  • I have no intention of selling this house.

  • (adj) intentional: có ý định trước, có dự định trước, có chủ tâm
  • I apologize for the omission of your name from the list. It was not intentional.
    • absent /ˈæb.sənt/ (adj) vắng mặt, nghỉ
  • The teacher called the roll to see if any students were absent.
    - Cô giáo điểm danh để xem có em học sinh nào vắng mặt hay không?
  • (phrase) absent from: vắng mặt tại địa điểm được đề cập
  • John has been absent from school/work for three days now.
  • He was absent from work for two weeks.
  • Vietnam was absent from Melbourne International Film Festival.
  • She has been absent from work due to illness.
    		•
    inform /ɪnˈfɔrm/ (v) thông báo, báo cho (ai) biết
  • The name of the dead man will not be released until his relatives have been informed.
  • I'm happy to inform you that you have been promoted to captain.
  • (n) information: thông tin, tin tức
  • The police are appealing to the public for any information about the missing girl.
  • I read an interesting bit/piece of information in the newspaper.
  • Please contact our agent in Spain for further information.
  • (n) informer: chỉ điểm, mật thám
  • Most police informers receive a reward for their information.
  • (n) informant: người cung cấp thông tin, nhân chứng
  • Our survey is based on information from over 200 informants.
  • (adj) informative: cung cấp nhiều thông tin, mang nhiều thông tin
  • This is an interesting and highly informative book.
  • I commend Ms. Orth on writing such an informative article.
    		•
    announce /əˈnaʊns/ (v) thông báo, loan báo (bản tin, đài, loa phát thanh...)
  • My sister surprised everyone by announcing she was leaving her job.
  • She announced the winner of the competition to an excited audience.
  • They announced the death of their mother in the local paper.
  • (n) announcement: cáo thị, thông cáo

    • Một số câu trích trong part 3-4:
  • Where most likely is the announcement being made?
  • According to the announcement, how can listeners learn about updates?
  • What is the announcement about?
  • According to the announcement, why should listeners visit the Hartman Web site?
    Cặp đồng nghĩa Inform - Announce xuất hiện rất nhiều trong các đề TOEIC. Những cặp từ đồng nghĩa thường xuất hiện trong Part 7 với dạng câu hỏi từ này ở đoạn xyz đồng nghĩa với từ ở đáp án nào dưới đây.
