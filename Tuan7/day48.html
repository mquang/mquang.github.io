Từ / Cụm từ Phiên âm Nghĩa Ví dụ
reinforce /ˌriː.ɪnˈfɔːrs/ (v) tăng cường, củng cố, gia cố, làm vững chắc thêm, tiếp viện
- to make a structure or material stronger, especially by adding another material to it
- to make a feeling, an idea, etc. stronger
- to provide an army with more soldiers or weapons to make it stronger
  • The bad weather forecast only reinforces our decision to leave early tomorrow.
  • The levees will need to be reinforced.
  • The pockets on my jeans are reinforced with double stitching.
    • YouTube: Home Repair Tutor
  • (n) reinforcement: sự tăng cường, sự củng cố, sự tiếp viện (the act of making something stronger)
  • The bridge is in need of reinforcement.
  • We need to prevent enemy reinforcements from reaching the front line.
    		•
    strengthen /ˈstreŋ.θn/ (v) làm vững chắc, làm kiên cố, củng cố
  • Repairs are necessary to strengthen the bridge.
  • They have been strengthening their border defences in preparation for war.
  • The concrete is strengthened with steel rods.
  • She showed me some exercises for strengthening my lower back.
    		•
    intensify /ɪnˈten.sɪ.faɪ/ (v) tăng cường, mãnh liệt, dữ dội thêm
    1. [no object]: to become stronger or more extreme : to become more intense
    2. [+ object]: to make (something) stronger or more extreme : to make (something) more intense
  • Violence intensified during the night.
  • Fighting around the capital has intensified in the last few hours.
    • YouTube: Krissy Cela
  • (adj) intense: gắt, dữ dội
  • He suddenly felt an intense pain in his back.
  • intense heat/pain
    		•
    heighten /ˈhaɪ.t̬ən/ (v) tăng thêm
    syn: intensify
  • Increased levels of fat in the diet could heighten the risk of cancer.
  • Tension has heightened after the recent bomb attack.
  • Tensions between the two groups have heightened.
    		•
    enhance /ɪnˈhæns/ (v) tăng, tăng cường (to improve the quality, amount, or strength of something)
  • High productivity lowers costs and enhances value.
  • The marinade enhances the flavor of the fish.
  • Garlic enhances the flavor of meals.
  • Can we enhance the image?
  • The image has been digitally enhanced to show more detail.



    • Youtube: Beauty Within
  • (n) enhancement: sự tăng cường, bổ sung
  • We have made many enhancements to the software that will strengthen system security.



    		•
    boost /buːst/ (v) tăng, nâng lên, đưa lên
    - to improve or increase something
    - to lift someone or something by pushing from below
    (n) sự tăng cường, bổ sung (năng lượng), sự nâng lên
    		 (v)
  • The movie helped boost her screen career.
  • She boosted the boy onto his father's shoulders.
  • To boost my chances of success on the math test, I studied a whole three weeks in advance to make sure I would ace it.
  • We need a big win to boost our confidence.


    • (n)
  • I needed a boost to keep me awake in the morning, so I drank two cups of coffee.
  • If there’s one thing that gives me the boost I need to make it through the day, it’s the encouragement of my friends and family.
  • I need a boost to get over the wall.
