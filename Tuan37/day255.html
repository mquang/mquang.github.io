intelligence /ɪnˈtel.ə.dʒəns/ (n) khả năng hiểu biết, trí thông minh (the ability to learn, understand and think in a logical way about things)
  • an intelligence test
  • IQ
  • Is it really possible to measure intelligence?
  • Most people of average intelligence would find this task quite difficult.
    		•
    criterion /kraɪˈtɪr.i.ən/ (n) tiêu chí (a standard that you use to judge something or make a decision about something)
    (n-s) criteria
  • What criteria are used for assessing a student's ability?
  • Academic ability is not the sole criterion for admission to the college.
  • There’s a list of criteria that you must meet in order to pass your driving test.
    • YT: ABC Education
    indicator /ˈɪn.dɪ.keɪ.t̬ər/ (n)
    1. dấu hiệu nhận biết, dấu hiệu cho thấy (a sign that shows you what something is like or how a situation is changing)

    2. đèn xi nhan (a light on a vehicle that flashes to show that the vehicle is going to turn left or right)
    		 1.
  • Rising sales of the company’s newest tech product is a good indicator that the phone will be a best-seller.
  • Phenolphthalein be used as the indicator.

    • 2.
  • Her right-hand indicator was flashing.
    		•
    gifted /ˈɡɪf.tɪd/ (adj) tài năng, có năng khiếu
    (syn) talented
  • a gifted
  • a special class for gifted children
  • He's very gifted at maths.
    		•
    notion /ˈnoʊ.ʃən/ (n) ý kiến, quan điểm, quan niệm
  • The traditional notion of marriage goes back thousands of years.
  • They were not familiar with the notion that women should have equal status with men.
    		•
    pan out /pæn/ (phrasal verb) tiến triển tốt, khả quan (to work out, to turn out well)
  • Their attempt to start a new business didn't pan out.
  • I'm glad to see that your business plan has panned out.
    		•
    kinesthetic intelligence /ˌkɪn.əsˈθet̬.ɪk/ (n) trí thông minh thể chất, những người có trí thông minh dạng này thường thích hoạt động hoặc biểu diễn, giỏi làm việc với các bộ phận cơ thể
    spatial intelligence /ˈspeɪ.ʃəl/ (n) trí thông minh cảm nhận không gian, giỏi làm việc với các vật thể trong không gian
    Bạn có biết: có 9 loại dạng thức thông minh, bạn nào quan tâm có thể tìm hiểu thêm trên Google.
    artistic /ɑːrˈtɪs.tɪk/ (adj)
    1. (liên quan đến) nghệ thuật (relating to art)

    2. có năng khiếu về nghệ thuật
  • showing a natural skill or pleasure in art, especially being able to paint or draw well
  • able to create or enjoy art

    		•
  • She comes from a very artistic family.
  • an artistic eye
  • (n) artist:
    1. họa sĩ, nghệ nhân     (a person who paints, draws, or makes sculptures)

    2. diễn viên, nghệ sĩ (an artist also an actor, musician, dancer, or other performer)
  • a artist
  • Monet is one of my favourite artists
