    popularity /ˌpɑː.pjəˈler.ə.t̬i/ (n) sự nổi tiếng, được nhiều người biết đến, nể, yêu quý, ưa chuộng (the state of being liked, enjoyed or supported by a large number of people)
  • the increasing popularity of organic food
  • Her obvious popularity made me feel inferior.
  • Everyone knew Ms. Campbell in the community due to her popularity as a great teacher at the school.
  • (adj) popular:
    1. được nhiều người ưa thích, yêu thích     (liked, enjoyed, or supported by many people)

    2. phổ biến, phần đông, số đông
    (of ideas, beliefs and opinions) shared by a large number of people
    • 1.
  • She's the most popular teacher in school.
  • The potato is the most popular vegetable in Britain.
  • It's the good weather that makes Spain such a popular tourist destination.

    • 2.
  • By popular demand, the tour has been extended by two weeks.
  • Contrary to popular belief, women cause fewer road accidents than men.
  • Contrary to popular belief, the existing safety regulations were adequate.
  • (v) popularize: phổ biến, phổ cập
    + to make something well known and liked
    + to make a difficult subject or idea able to be easily understood by ordinary people who have no special knowledge about it
