    speaking of something (phrase) nói đến, tiện đang nói đến ... (related to the subject being discussed)
    You can say speaking of something that has just been mentioned as a way of introducing a new topic which has some connection with that thing.
  • Casey is at a birthday party – speaking of birthdays, Abe’s is Friday.
  • A: "We need to get the tires checked on the car soon."
    B: "Speaking of tires, I need a new one for my bike, too."
