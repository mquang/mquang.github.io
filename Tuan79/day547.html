warm up /wɔːrm/ (ph.v)
1. khởi động, làm nóng toàn thân

2. làm nóng máy (if you warm up a machine or an engine, you switch it on and wait for a little while until it is ready to be used)

3. bắt đầu nóng lên, náo nhiệt, sôi động (if an event or situation warms up, or if something warms it up, it starts to become enjoyable, interesting, or busy)
