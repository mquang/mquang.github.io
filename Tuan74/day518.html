as if/as though (conjunction)
1. như thể, như kiểu, cứ như là (giả định rõ ràng là không đúng sự thật)

2. có vẻ như (giả định có khả năng là đúng thật)
 1.
  • I felt as though I'd been lying in the sun for hours.
  • They stared at me as if I was crazy.
  • He acts as if/as though he knew the answers.

    • 2.
  • She looks as though she lives here.
  • He looks as if he knows the answer.
  • He seems as if he hasn't slept for days.
    • YT: BBC Learning English
    soon /suːn/ (adv) sớm, sớm thôi, sớm nha (không lâu kể từ sau thời điểm nói) (in a short time from now)
  • Get well soon.
  • See you soon.
  • I shall send you the details soon.
  • I am planning to start a business soon.
  • Let's go home. It will be dark soon.
  • We’ll have to leave soon. (We’ll have to leave after a short time from now.)
  • (adv, adj) early:
    1. sớm hơn mốc thời gian đã định hoặc sớm hơn thông thường     (before the appointed or the usual time)

    2. vẫn còn quá sớm, mới chỉ là giai đoạn khởi đầu (used to emphasize that something has just begun, especially when you do not know how it will develop)

    3. (dạng so sánh hơn) trước đó
    (adj) ở khoảng thời gian đầu (những năm)
    • (adv1)
  • Please come to office early tomorrow.
  • Sheena arrived early.
  • We reached airport very early and had a nice breakfast in the lounge.
  • We’ll have to leave early. (We’ll have to leave before the usual or planned time, e.g. early in the morning, so that we do not arrive late.)
  • The best rooms go to those who book earliest.

    • (adv2)
  • It’s too early to say what will happen.
  • The project is still in its very early stages.
  • It is too early to predict whether the unemployment rate has peaked.

    • (adv3)
  • As I earlier...


    • (adj)
  • Sheena’s in her early thirties.

    hear /hɪər/ (v)
    1. không chủ định nghe mà vẫn nghe được

    2. được nghe tin, hay tin, báo tin, biết tin (to be told information about something)

    3. xét xử (to listen to all the facts in a case in a court of law in order to make a legal decision)
    hear - heard - heard
    1.
  • Suddenly I heard a noise. Someone was in the garden.
  • Did you hear the thunder last night?

    • 2.
  • Have you heard ?
  • I hear that you're leaving.
  • I was sorry to hear about your accident.
  • I was surprised to hear (that) he was married.
  • When I heard the news, I was devastated.
  • (v) listen (to): chú tâm nghe
  • I listened very carefully to what she said and wrote it all down.
  • Listen to me! I have something important to tell you.
    • YT: BBC Learning English
    see /siː/ (v)
    1. nhìn thấy thứ gì đó bằng khả năng tự nhiên, bản năng; nhìn thấy gì đó 1 cách tình cờ

    2. xem (phim tại rạp, buổi trình diễn, biểu diễn, thi đấu trực tiếp mà không phải qua màn ảnh) (We use see, not watch, when we talk about being at sports matches or public performances, such as films, theatre and dramas.)

    say /seɪ/ (v) nói (không cần object theo sau, khi có object theo sau cần kết hợp với to)
    say - said - said
    Chú ý phát âm dễ gây nhầm lẫn: says và said
    Common collocation with say:
    Say:
    + hi, hello, goodbye
    + yes, no
    + something
    + sorry
    + thank you
  • She said he was her best friend.
  • He said that he couldn't do the job by himself.
  • The doctors say (that) it will take him a few weeks to recover.
  • Tom asked if I wanted to go to a party and I said yes.
  • John said to Sara that she was beautiful in her red dress.
  • Do you know what she said to him?
  • I've got something to say to you.
  • (v) tell: nói (cần object theo sau)
    • tell - told - told
    Common collocation with tell (những trường hợp này có thể không cần object theo sau cũng được)
    Tell:
    + the truth
    + a lie
    + a secret
    + a story
    + the time
  • She told me he was her best friend.
  • He told them the truth, but they didn't believe him.
  • She's only five—she hasn't learnt to tell the time yet.

    speak /spiːk/ (v)
    1. nói chuyện (trịnh trọng, nghiêm túc)

    2. nói về bài giảng, bài diễn thuyết, bài thuyết trình

    3. nói (liên quan đến phạm trù ngôn ngữ)
    speak - spoke - spoken
    1.
  • Can I speak to Dan please?
  • I need to speak to the manager.

    • 2.
  • Dr Graham Foster will speak about the history of the region.
  • He spoke about the importance of taking exercise and having a good diet.

    • 3.
  • How many languages do you speak?
  • (v) talk:
    1. nói chuyện (bình thường, với bạn bè, không trịnh trọng, nghiêm túc bằng speak)

    2. nói về bài giảng, bài diễn thuyết, bài thuyết trình (không trịnh trọng, nghiêm túc bằng speak)

    3. nói chuyện, bàn bạc (để tìm hướng giải quyết)
    • 1.
  • We were just talking about Gareth's new girlfriend.
  • She talks to her mother on the phone every week.

    • 2.
  • Kyle is going to talk us through the benefits of the software and then Liz will talk about the marketing plan.

    • 3.
  • The two sides have agreed to talk.
  • This situation can't go on. We need to talk.


    • say và tell:
    + say đề cập đến người nói và thông tin người nói đưa ra
    + tell đề cập đến người nói, người nghe và thông tin người nói đưa ra
    + say không cần object theo sau, tell theo sau bởi object; nếu muốn say theo sau bởi object thì cần kết hợp say với to
    speak và talk:
    + người nói và người nghe (người được người nói nhắc đến) cùng tham gia vào cuộc đối thoại
    + nói về bài giảng, bài diễn thuyết, bài thuyết trình
    + speak trịnh trọng hơn talk
