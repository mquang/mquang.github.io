        Luyện ngheI've Found an Old Treasure Map in the Attic

        Hi! My name is Ellie, and I found a real treasure map in the attic of our house. I had to go through hard trials to find the treasure. But in order not to spoil the intrigue, I will tell you everything from the beginning.

        This story started when my family decided to move into the spacious but old house of my grandfather who had passed away several years ago. I was happy about this decision since I’ve never liked living in a noisy city. There was one more reason to be glad as my best friend Mindy and I were about to become neighbors.

        To be honest, there was one unpleasant aspect in this move, and it lied in tidying up this huge house. I decided to clean the attic because it was possible to find something interesting there. In addition, I called Mindy for help and the cleaning process became much faster. My friend and I were looking through the old books and newspapers, admiring ancient clothes, and cleaning up all the trash, while saving rare things for ourselves. Sifting through another stack of books, I came across a small leather-bound notebook. I really liked it and put it aside together with other things that I wanted to keep.

        After opening my notebook in the evening, I was shocked because it was a real traveler's diary. Forgetting that I was supposed to sleep, I began to read it page by page. As it turned out, he was born here and traveled all over the world. Flipping through the diary to the middle of it, I noticed a map that was between the pages. It was a piece of parchment made of thick yellowish paper folded many times. An intricate route was drawn on it with a red pencil and it was interrupted in some places. It also included various marks and inscriptions.

        After carefully studying the map, I suddenly came across something because of which my heart almost jumped out of my chest. Grandpa's house was marked on the map. It was written next to it that the map would lead to all the treasures that the traveler was able to collect. I was so excited by this idea that all night, I could not close my eyes thinking about the treasure.

        In the morning, I immediately rushed to Mindy and showed her the map. I decided to postpone reading the diary for a while so as not to waste time and find the treasure faster. A friend was shocked by what she saw. She immediately agreed to help me in my search.

        The path to the treasure started from an old oak tree, which was not far from my grandfather's house, so we immediately headed towards it. We saw a path next to it, and it seemed to us that it would be easy to find the treasure. But then the map led us to a completely different path. Probably, it was drawn a long time ago, so the route indicated on the map was already overgrown with bushes. While making our way through the branches with difficulty, we approached a large forest lake. The route on the map ran right along it, but we decided to go around.

        At first, we felt very smart and cunning because it was not a very difficult task to get around the lake. But when we almost reached the right place, the path was blocked by the landslide stones and a high cliff. Without losing hope, we decided to go on the other side of the lake, but, as it turned out, there was a viscous swamp. Suddenly we had such despair that we could hardly hold back our tears. We felt helpless and stupid because we were not able to handle even the first task of the map.

        For over an hour, we’ve been thinking about how we could cross the lake. Only then we pulled ourselves together and took a closer look at the map seeing a boat drawn there. I took out the traveler's diary and quickly flipped through the pages until I found the same drawing. In the diary, it was written that we could cross the lake using something that was hidden nearby. It was also mentioned that dead trees would help us move. This sounded creepy, but we decided not to back down.

        While searching the shore, Mindy discovered a hidden old raft that miraculously did not fall apart in so many years. After that, I immediately solved the riddle and broke off two long branches from the fallen tree. We sailed on the water constantly being afraid that the raft was about to fall apart, but, fortunately, it bore our weight.

        Then the map led Mindy and me through a small field to the place where the forest was cut down a long time ago. Then the route on it was interrupted by the mark of the house. After finding the same mark in the diary, we learned that in the forester's barn nearby, there was hidden something that would definitely come in handy in our search for treasures. We have been wandering around the edge of the forest for several hours, but we could not find any house or barn. This time despair was mixed with anger. All these trials left by the traveler began to drive us crazy. In addition, we started doubting that the treasure was actually buried in that place.

        We were about to give up, but then Mindy noticed something among the trees. It was a rickety barn covered with plants. We hardly made our way to it and found several rusty shovels there. The end of the route was not far away since our destination was the empty land behind a neighboring hill. The path to the hill ran through a large field. And, when we were already far from the forest, we heard the rumbling of thunder. A huge thundercloud was moving towards us from behind the forest. We literally had nowhere to hide from the storm. Being terrified, Mindy and I were both gripped by panic. We rushed to the hill without looking back, and only at the last moment changed our minds to hide from the storm on the tree at its top.

        As it turned out, it had saved our lives. We sat down at the foot of the hill hoping that the storm would pass quickly. When it covered us, a heavy downpour began, and lightning struck right into that tree of the hill, which frightened us very much. The rain has lasted for no more than half an hour, but when it ended, we were soaked to the bone together with our map.

        The mark at the end of the route was erased, and we could no longer recognize the hint. The path ended in an empty area behind a hill, but instead of an empty field, at that place, there was a recently built farm. I could not believe my eyes and just burst into tears because of anger and resentment. The diary was my only hope, but it didn't help either. We managed to find only a clue about a lonely pine tree, but it was not clear where to look for it. The last pages of the diary were just torn out.

        We decided to go around the farm and it turned out to be the right decision. In the backyard of the farm, there was growing a lonely pine tree, which has been left since those times, probably, for decorative purposes. However, we faced a problem. There were people on the farm, and we could not just enter their territory. Mindy and I had a serious argument about whether to try to take the treasure or not. I didn't want the path that we had traveled to be in vain, and Mindy didn't want to take risks for the sake of the treasure that might be absent there. I hardly persuaded my friend to help me, but she just agreed to watch the farmers from afar.

        It was already the evening, and many farmers were having rest, so I waited for a moment and made my way to the backyard. It was a very risky thing to do, but I had to find the treasure buried under the pine tree. After making sure that no one was around, I started digging. I did not know how deeply the treasure was hidden, but after digging quite a deep hole, I realized that I needed to look for it elsewhere. So I have dug several holes around the pine tree and spent a whole hour to do it. Finally, the shovel bumped into something solid, and then my heart sank, and tears of joy came to my eyes. It was a small old chest. I had to crawl to the fence holding a chest in my hands so that the people who came out onto the terrace would not notice me. Because of fear, I was ready to turn into a stone, but I continued crawling and getting dirty in the mud.

        All in all, the treasure was in our hands. A key was tied to the chest so we opened it impatiently and with trepidation. The rapidly beating heart sank when, instead of diamonds and gold, we saw different kinds of trinkets inside the chest. Apart from different strange things, there was a very old photograph with smiling children, and among them, I recognized my grandfather. Torn pages of a diary were next to it. From them, I learned that my grandfather, when he was still a child, came up with this whole story about a traveler. He and his friends drew this map and hid the treasure. To be honest, Mindy and I were terribly upset. Being in despair, we took out all the trinkets from the chest and saw a handful of old coins at the bottom. We did not attach any importance to them, so we put everything in our backpack and went home in complete silence, without saying a word.

        When I told my parents about the search for the treasure at home and showed the coins, dad almost jumped in his chair in surprise. He examined them carefully, and then said that they were from the limited edition and in our times, they were considered an expensive find for collectors. The price of each of them was approximately $100,000. However, we decided not to sell them, but to keep them in memory of my grandfather.
