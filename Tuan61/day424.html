go around (phrasal verb)
1. đủ cho mọi người (to be enough for everyone to have one or some)

2. đến thăm, đến chơi (to visit a person or a place)

3. lan truyền, lây lan (if something such as an illness or a piece of news is going around, people are giving or telling it to each other)
also go round
1.
  • There aren't enough chairs to go around.
  • Are there enough leaflets to go round?

    • 2.

    • 3.
  • There’s a story going around the office that you’re thinking of leaving.
  • A rumour was going around that I was having an affair with my boss.
