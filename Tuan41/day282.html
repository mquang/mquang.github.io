turn up (ph.v) 1. xuất hiện
1.1. đến (to arrive)

1.2. tìm thấy (to be found, especially by chance, after having been lost or searched for)

1.3. (cơ hội) tới, xuất hiện (if an opportunity or situation turns up, it happens, especially when you are not expecting it)
2. tăng (âm lượng, nhiệt độ) (to increase the sound, heat, etc. of a piece of equipment)
 1.1.
  • We arranged to meet at 7.30, but she never turned up.

    • 1.2.
  • Eventually my watch turned up in a coat pocket.

    • 1.3.
  • Don’t worry, I’m sure a job will turn up soon.

  • Could you turn the TV up?
  • Turn the oven up to 220.
