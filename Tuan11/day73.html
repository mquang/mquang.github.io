inventive /ɪnˈven.tɪv/ (adj) có óc sáng tạo, đầy sáng tạo
- (especially of people) able to think of new and interesting ideas
- (of ideas) new and interesting
(syn) creative
  • Tom is inventive.
  • You're inventive.
  • (v) invent:
    1. phát minh, sáng chế     (to design and/or create something that has never been made before)
    2. hư cấu, bịa ra (to create a reason, excuse, story, etc. that is not true, usually to deceive someone)
    • 1.
  • Alexander Graham Bell invented the telephone in 1876.
  • The telephone was invented by Alexander Graham Bell.
  • Do you know who invented the microscope?
  • Edison invented the light bulb.

    • 2.
  • But I didn't invent the story - everything I told you is true.
  • She invented a clever excuse.
  • They invented a very convincing alibi.
  • (n) invention: phát minh, sáng chế
    • 1.
  • The dishwasher is a wonderful invention.

    • 2.
  • The world changed rapidly after the invention of the phone.
